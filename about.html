Jen's Story

Some people find crystals. Jen believes crystals found her. It started with a single amethyst point discovered at a Tucson gem show over two decades ago — a deep purple specimen that she couldn't put down until it was wrapped in tissue paper and safely in her bag. That was the beginning.

"I didn't set out to open a shop. I set out to share something I genuinely love — and the shop just grew out of that."

Over the years, Jen's collection grew from a single shelf to a dedicated room, from a room to a storage unit, and eventually to the shop she'd always quietly dreamed of. The Lavender Butterfly opened its doors in Sierra Vista's beloved mall, and from the very first day, it felt like the community had been waiting for it.

Every piece in the shop has been hand-selected by Jen at gem shows, from trusted mineral dealers, and through relationships built over years with miners and importers who share her standard: the stone has to be exceptional. With over 330 unique SKUs on the floor at any given time, no two visits look alike.

The Journey

How We Got Here

The Beginning
The First Stone

Jen picks up her first crystal specimen at a Tucson gem show — a deep violet amethyst point that sparks what would become a two-decade obsession with the earth's natural formations.

The Collection Years
A Room Becomes a Library

The collection fills shelves, then rooms. Jen develops relationships with miners, importers, and dealers — learning to identify quality, authenticity, and the quiet energy that distinguishes a good stone from a great one.

The Dream Takes Shape
Community Roots in Sierra Vista

Sierra Vista becomes home. Jen begins sharing crystals with friends, neighbors, and anyone who would listen. The informal exchange of stones grows into something bigger than a hobby.

The Lavender Butterfly
Opening Day at Sierra Vista Mall

The Lavender Butterfly opens its doors. The name came naturally — the butterfly for transformation, lavender for the soft purple hues that run through so many of Jen's favorite stones. The community embraces it from day one.

Today
330+ Pieces & Growing

The shop carries over 330 unique one-of-a-kind pieces at any given time. New stock arrives regularly from gem shows, curated sourcing trips, and Jen's ever-growing network of trusted suppliers around the world.

What We're About

The Lavender Butterfly Way

🔍
Quality Over Quantity

Jen turns down more pieces than she buys. Every stone on the floor has earned its place. If it doesn't pass her standard, it doesn't come home with her.

🌍
Ethically Sourced

We work with suppliers we know and trust. Origin matters. We can tell you where almost every specimen in the shop came from and who brought it to the surface.

🤝
No Pressure, Just Discovery

Whether you're a first-time buyer or a seasoned collector, you'll never feel rushed or talked into something. We'd rather you find exactly the right stone than just any stone.

📖
Always Learning

Jen attends multiple gem shows a year, reads constantly, and still gets excited when she encounters a formation she hasn't seen before. That curiosity is what keeps the shop alive.

Genuine Specimens
