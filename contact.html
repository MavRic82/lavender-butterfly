Common Questions

Good to Know

🏷️
Do you have prices online?

Each piece is one-of-a-kind and priced individually. We don't publish prices online, but reach out about any item — we're happy to share pricing and photos.

📦
Do you ship?

We primarily serve our local Sierra Vista community in person. Contact us to discuss special arrangements for shipping on select pieces.

⏸️
Can you hold a piece for me?

Absolutely. Call us or send a message and we can hold most items for 24–48 hours while you plan your visit. Payment on pickup.

💳
What payment do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and mobile payments. Gift certificates are also available — a beautiful gift for any occasion.
